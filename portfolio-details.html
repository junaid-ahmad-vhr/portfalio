Abstrect

Age estimation from facial images is attracting and increasing interest due to its many potential applications. YOLO is an algorithm that based on convolutional neural networks to provide real-time object detection. This algorithm is popular because of its speed and accuracy. We will use yolo algorithm to predict age of persons based on facial features. The first step will be to label the images, for this purpose we can use different available tools like LabelImg. Then we will setup environment and train our model and predict the age from facial images.Several deep learning-based methods have been proposed to tackle this task; however, In this work, we propose a system based on YOLOv4 to perform age estimation in real-time. Also, to make the model more robust, Model was trained on the UTK Face Age Dataset. The results obtained in the contest are promising, and are strengthened by the lightness of the overall system which in fact is not only effective but also efficient.
